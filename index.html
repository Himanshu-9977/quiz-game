How to Play:

  • Start the Quiz: Click the 'Start' button on this screen to begin your quiz journey. The quiz will contain random questions related to 'NEPAL'.
  • Answer Questions: Each question will appear one at a time. You'll have four options to choose from. Click on the option you think is correct.
  • Scoring: Each correct answer earns you one point. There are no penalties for wrong answers, so guess if you're unsure!
  • Progress: Your current question number and total score will be displayed as you progress through the quiz.
  • End of Quiz: The game concludes when you have answered all questions. Your final score will be displayed.

Additional Rules:

  • Timing: There is no time limit per question, but the total time taken to complete the quiz will be recorded. Try to answer as quickly as possible!
  • Review: Once answered, questions cannot be revisited during the same session. Make sure you're confident in your choice before moving on!
  • Randomness: Questions are presented in a random order each time the quiz is started to provide a unique challenge.

Tips for success:

  • Take your time to think through each question. Although speed is a factor, accuracy is more crucial.
  • Familiarize yourself with a wide range of topics to better prepare for the diverse questions in the quiz.
  • Enjoy the process! This is a great opportunity to test your knowledge and learn something new.
  • Start your Quiz Adventure Now and Aim for the Top Score!
